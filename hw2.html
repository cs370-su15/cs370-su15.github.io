How to get Unstuck

In section, we covered the "cognitive processes" dimension of Bloom's Taxonomy. However, there is much more to Bloom's Taxonomy than that. In this assignment, we'll focus on the "knowledge dimension", which turns our pyramid model into a 2 dimensional Qbert-esqe shape. (+1 to you if you understood the reference)

Read Quick Guide on Getting Unstuck. Write your thoughts on this guide in a google doc and answer these questions::

Now that you've finished CS61A, reflect on what parts of this guide would be useful to you if you were in the class.

  • Do you see yourself using these tips to work through problems on your own?
  • Can you see yourself using these tips to guide students through a problem? Why or why not?
  • Do you think a student currently in CS61A could make good use of this guide? Why or why not?

Fixed vs. Growth Mindset
