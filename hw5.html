TA Shadowing

Big idea: Find a discussion for CS61A and crash it this coming week, or next week, and write up feedback for how the TA is doing. Put that Feedback in your Folder.

Quick Note: Thursday Sections are not organized for this week since they are just intended to be midterm review. If you cannot attend a discussion on Tuesday, wait until next week.

Details:
  • Find a section (time, date, place, and TA) that you can make. Make a post in the followups saying what section you are going to attend. Check to see if no one else has selected this section. There may be at most two of you in any one section. So if you pick a section early, you can avoid the rush. :P
  • Make a followup here saying that you're going to that section and then send the a TA a heads up (an email is fine) saying that you're going to crash, and if that's okay. A template is provided below.
  • Observe the TA in section. Spend at least one hour in the section. Create a feedback form and use it to rate how well the TA is doing. To help you, below are some examples of feedback forms students have created in the past.
  • I'm looking for one page with detailed descriptions of how the TA led section, how that impacted students, and what you thought of it. Use examples and mentioning the things we've seen in class so far. Let me know if you need more guidance in followups.
