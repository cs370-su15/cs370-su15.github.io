Bloom's Taxonomy

In section, we covered the "cognitive processes" dimension of Bloom's Taxonomy. However, there is much more to Bloom's Taxonomy than that. In this assignment, we'll focus on the "knowledge dimension", which turns our pyramid model into a 2 dimensional Qbert-esqe shape. (+1 to you if you understood the reference)

Read 2D Bloom's Taxonomy and do the following:

In a bDrive document:

Pick a code writing problem. You may use discussions, past midterms, guerrilla section worksheets, etc. Check the old course websites. Don't forget to link to the original source in your doc.

For that problem, describe the necessary steps to solve it. Be as explicit as possible. Each step should be designed to guide a student along toward the solution. Pay special attention to common questions or misconceptions with that problem.

Then, for each step, label where it falls on the new 2D Bloom's Taxonomy (as described in the above link). We're trying to answer the question: What sort of mastery do the students need to know in order to follow that step?
